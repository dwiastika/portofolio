YUP'S GURT PROJECT

Business Plan Competition : Yup's Gurt

Comprehensive business planning for a yogurt-based beverage startup focusing on product differentiation, target market analysis, and sustainable value creation.

Role: Participant Program: Business Plan Competition Year: 2020

Project Overview

This project focused on developing an innovative yogurt product to address low yogurt consumption in Indonesia, despite its proven health benefits. Consumer research indicated that sour taste perception and limited product differentiation reduced consumer interest.

Leveraging healthy lifestyle trends and digital marketing strategies.

The Product Innovation Focused On:

  • Reducing sour taste perception through the addition of crunchy cereal textures
  • Offering multiple flavor variants, including Froot Loops, Honey Stars, Corn Flakes, and Milo Crunch
  • Using natural sweeteners such as honey and fruits
  • Creating a yogurt product that is both healthy and appealing to children and young consumers

Value Proposition

  • Innovative Product Concept: Yogurt with crunchy cereal toppings for a unique and enjoyable experience.
  • Health-Focused Ingredients: Naturally sweetened with honey and fruits, supporting nutritional value and local farmers.

SWOT Analysis

Strengths

  • Innovative and healthy product
  • Variety of toppings and flavors
  • Affordable pricing
  • Uses natural ingredients (supports local farmers and natural sweeteners)

Weaknesses

  • Short shelf life (1 day)
  • Home-based production scale
  • Limited distribution (only in Jakarta)
  • Reliant on delivery services (GoFood and GrabFood)

Opportunities

  • Rising trend of a healthy lifestyle
  • Influencers support easier promotion
  • Affordable price encourages trial
  • High nutritional value & mood boosters are attractive

Threats

  • Competition from established yogurt brands
  • Products easily copied by competitors
  • Fluctuating prices of milk & fruits
  • Rapidly changing food trends

Key Contributions

  • Conducted a comprehensive market analysis using SWOT to identify strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats relevant to product development and market positioning.
  • Developed a clear product differentiation strategy, positioning the yogurt-and-cereal combination as a unique value proposition and competitive advantage.
  • Built a strong value proposition emphasizing health benefits, convenience, and a new taste experience tailored to millennial consumers.
  • Designed product mockups to visualize packaging and product concepts for pitching, stakeholder communication, and idea validation.
