GenBI Jakarta

GenBI (Generasi Baru Indonesia) Jakarta is a community of Bank Indonesia scholarship recipients in the Jakarta region that operates under the direct supervision of Bank Indonesia. The community aims to develop young individuals as Bank Indonesia frontliners, agents of change, and future leaders across various fields.
