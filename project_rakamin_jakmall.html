PROJECT CASE STUDY

Marketing Plan for Partner Acquisition & Retention at Jakmall

Strategic Campaign Planning and Performance Optimization

Role: Campaign and Digital Performance Officer Company: Jakmall x Rakamin Academy Year: 2025

Project Overview

Jakmall plans to launch a campaign in January 2024 to acquire 500 new partners for the Jakmall Partner Program through digital advertising, with the main objectives of acquisition and retention.

Campaign Detail

“Radiance Partner Program”

The word “Radiance” reflects the core values of the Health & Beauty category: cleanliness, glow, and confidence. This program is designed to give partners the opportunity to “shine” by building a sustainable dropship business with Jakmall.

Why Health & Beauty Category?

  • Health & Beauty sales in Q1 2024 increased by 42% compared to Q1 2023, indicating strong and growing market demand in Indonesia’s digital ecosystem.
  • Health & Beauty products are consumable goods, meaning customers tend to make repeat purchases almost every month — ideal for long-term dropship partners.
  • Wide product variety, ranging from skincare to supplements, allowing partners to adjust product selection based on their target market segment.

Hashtag: #RadiancePartnerProgram, #BeautyBossJakmall, #CantikItuBisnis

Key Insight

  • High-intent traffic contributes disproportionately to total partner acquisition.
  • Retargeting audiences show stronger conversion efficiency compared to cold traffic.
  • Early partner activation plays a critical role in long-term retention.

Optimization Strategy

  • Prioritize budget on channels with the strongest contribution to partner acquisition.
  • Focus optimization efforts on early-funnel activation and onboarding.
  • Align performance metrics with long-term partner lifetime value.
