PROJECT CASE STUDY

Digital Marketing Performance Analysis

Campaign analysis and optimization to improve digital performance and audience engagement.

Role: Digital Marketing Company: Jakmall x Rakamin Year: 2025

Project Overview

Jakmall plans to launch a campaign in January 2024 to acquire 500 new partners for the Jakmall Partner Program through digital advertising, with the main objectives of acquisition and retention.

Campaign Detail

“Radiance Partner Program”

The word “Radiance” reflects the core values of the Health & Beauty category: cleanliness, glow, and confidence. This program is designed to give partners the opportunity to “shine” by building a sustainable dropship business with Jakmall.

Why Health & Beauty Category?

  • Health & Beauty sales in Q1 2024 increased by 42% compared to Q1 2023, indicating strong and growing market demand in Indonesia’s digital ecosystem.
  • Health & Beauty products are consumable goods, meaning customers tend to make repeat purchases almost every month — ideal for long-term dropship partners.
  • Wide product variety, ranging from skincare to supplements, allowing partners to adjust product selection based on their target market segment.

Hashtag: #RadiancePartnerProgram, #BeautyBossJakmall, #CantikItuBisnis

Target Audience

Demographics

  • Age: 18–40 years old
  • Gender: Male & Female
  • Work & Education: Students, fresh graduates, housewives, workers, freelancers

Pain Point

  • Limited Capital
  • Fear of financial loss due to unsold stock
  • Untrustworthy suppliers
  • Limited business experience

Psychographics

  • Female college students & fresh graduates looking for a side hustle
  • Digital savvy housewives
  • Small resellers who want to level up
  • Beauty enthusiasts who want to monetize their passiont

Value Proposition

  • Business without holding product inventory
  • One-time membership payment for lifetime access
  • Trending products with reliable suppliers
  • Relationship Manager support for business operations and problem solving

Geographics

  • Area: Big Cities & urban areas (Jakarta, Bandung, Surabaya, Medan, Makassar, Jogja)
