DIGITAL MARKETING PROJECT

BNSP Certification Training : Study Case Noona Closet

Digital marketing strategy development focusing on keyword research, content planning, and brand positioning for a Korean-inspired fashion label.

Role: Digital Marketing Program: BNSP Certification Training Year: 2024

Brand Overview

Noona Closet is a women’s fashion brand inspired by Korean style, positioning itself as a sustainable fashion choice with premium value. The brand targets modern women who value elegance, comfort, and quality.

Market Segmentation & Positioning

Target Audience

  • Women aged 18+
  • Middle to upper-class consumers
  • University students & professional women
  • Korean fashion enthusiasts

Brand Positioning

  • Premium & sustainable fashion
  • Versatile blazers for daily & office wear
  • Elegant yet comfortable design

Keyword Research

Keyword research was conducted using Google Suggest, Google Trends, and SEMrush to identify high-intent keywords related to Korean-style blazers.

Blazer Korea Blazer Wanita Blazer Korea Style Blazer Import Korea

Content Planning Strategy

  • Fashion inspiration & OOTD content
  • Educational posts on fabric quality
  • Promotion & bazaar announcements
  • Customer testimonials & reviews
  • Sustainable fashion education

Deliverables

Keyword Research Analysis
Content Pillar Framework
Instagram Content Plan
Landing Page Concept

Results & Learning

  • Clear content direction aligned with brand identity
  • Improved understanding of audience behavior
  • Stronger positioning in competitive fashion market
