DIGITAL MARKETING PROJECT

BNSP Certification Training : Study Case Noona Closet

Digital marketing strategy development focusing on keyword research, content planning, and brand positioning for a Korean-inspired fashion brand.

Role: Digital Marketing Program: BNSP Certification Training Year: 2024

Brand Overview

Noona Closet is a women’s fashion brand inspired by Korean style, positioning itself as a sustainable fashion choice with premium value. The brand targets modern women who value elegance, comfort, and quality.

Target Audience

Demographics

  • Age: > 18 Years
  • Gender: Female
  • Work & Education: Young professionals, office workers, and university students who value appearance and follow fashion trends
  • Income Level: Middle to upper class with strong purchasing power

Targeting

  • Korean fashion lovers
  • Professional working women
  • Sustainable fashion

Psychographics

  • Fashion enthusiasts
  • Conscious consumers who value sustainable fashion
  • Fans of Korean culture and Korean fashion style

Geographics

  • Area: Jakarta

Brand Positioning

  • Sustainable fashion with premium quality: Designed with sustainability while maintaining exceptional quality
  • Versatile blazers for everyday and profesional look: Stylish yet professional blazers, perfect for daily wear and office

Keyword Research

Keyword research was conducted using Google Suggest, Google Trends, and SEMrush to identify high-intent keywords related to Korean-style blazers.

Main Keyword :
Blazer Korea Wanita
Keyword Support :
Blazer Korea Blazer Korea Style Blazer Wanita Korea Terbaru

Key Finding

  • High search interest was identified for keywords such as “Blazer Korea”, “Blazer Wanita”, and “Blazer Korea Style”, indicating strong awareness-level demand.
  • More specific terms such as “Blazer wanita Korea terbaru” reflect higher consideration and purchase intent.
